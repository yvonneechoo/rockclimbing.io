Ground Up Climbing

Descriptions

  • Indoor and Outdoor lead climbing and bouldering
  • Teaches you the proper climbing techniques and body conditioning exercises
  • Sample the sport with a taster session, or take up the SNCS Rock Climbing Level 1 course to learn how to belay and climb with your friends.
  • If you want to challenge yourself, the outdoor Wall is one of the hardest overhangs in Singapore

I went to Ground up Climbing but found that it was rather challenging. I only tried the indoor section although there were indoor and outdoor section as I think the outdoor section is only for the more advanced climbers. The bouldering ones were much tougher than the rock climbing although I only manage to complete 2 whole columns for rock climbing as the rest were rather challenging. As a novice climber, I would recommend climb central as it is more beginner friendly. Nevertheless, it was still a great experience, and I had a lot of fun climbing. Great routes, friendly staffs and affordable gym membership. GU pretty much feels like a “kampung spirit” where everyone helps each other (including staffs) with climbing moves and trade betas. There're many auto belay lanes and you don't have to bring a partner to climb with. Bouldering area also include system walls and hang-boards for additional training. Also nice outdoor lead wall with roof section for those who want to challenge themselves further. Overall, a complete gym climbing experience with good foundation for progress in the sport.


Jane Tan | 20 December 2021
