Climb Central

Descriptions

  • No prior experience to scale the walls
  • Tallest indoor air-conditioned rock face in Singapore which goes up to 16-metres high.
  • Advanced climbing safety systems are in place to ensure even the most inexperienced climbers can clamber to new heights (as long as you're above the age of five)
  • There are 45 climbing lanes and 4-metre-high bouldering walls - in a safe and controlled environment

I went to Climb Central Funan with my family and we all had a lot of fun! Even my younger brother who was only 14 years old manage to have a lot of fun from climbing. I would say that they have a good mix of various difficulty level that accommodate to different people and thus I would highly recommend this as a family bonding activity! I really enjoy myself there and will definitely come back again


Yvonne Choo | 23 Nov 2019

If you want to have a great full body workout doing at your own pace and timing, this might be a good alternative .. doesn't matter if youre a beginner, novice or experienced climber. fee is valid for whole day stay inside the gym, situated inside the mall .. also there are auto-belays system so solo climbing is possible ... a recommended activity for the whole family, group of friends, even yourself!

I also like that several top-rope routes had auto-belay systems which made it more convenient to climb. Place is air-conditioned and comfortable which is great. Facilities are also very clean. Overall it's a great place to climb!
